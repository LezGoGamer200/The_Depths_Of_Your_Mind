Tips & Tricks!
An important part of any gaming website, & I have a few to share with you! The first being, you can find only one of each weapon, & once you find one, you can't find one anywhere else in the whole world of Dragons & Daydreaming. You can use this tid bit of information to find every other weapon in the game, except for the Legendary Dream Cleaver on dark peak mountain. Then go to dark peak, & the only weapon you can find there is the Dream Cleaver, therefore, if you get then weapon chance while searching, it is garunteed to be the Dream Cleaver. My second tip is to remember to sleep, & play some mini-games. They both heal you back to full health, & sleeping has some fun "cutscenes" for you to watch/read. That is all for now, have fun!
